Beshparmak

  • Time: 4 h 30 m
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Lamb 1 kg
Onions 3 onions
Carrots 200 g
Potatoes 500 g
Flour 500 g
Eggs 2 eggs
Water 200 ml
Salt to taste

Step 1: Boil the lamb in 3 liters of water, add salt and pepper. Cut the onions into rings, and the carrots and potatoes into slices.

Step 2: Prepare the dough by mixing flour, eggs, water, and salt. Roll out the dough and cut it into thin strips.

Step 3: Add the carrots and potatoes to the boiling lamb and cook for 30 minutes.

Step 4: Add the dough strips to the pot and cook until they float to the surface.

Step 5: Remove the lamb, vegetables, and dough from the pot. Serve the lamb on a large platter, topped with vegetables and dough strips.
